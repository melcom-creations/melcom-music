Cookie Settings & Tracking

When you visit my site for the first time, I ask for your consent for visitor analysis. Here is a simple explanation of what the options mean:

Accept all

If you choose this option, you help me a lot! A script from Statcounter will be loaded, which anonymously records which pages are visited. No personal data such as your name or email address is stored. I only see that "someone" was there and, for example, looked at a specific track. This helps me understand which of my works you like best.

Accept necessary

If you choose this, I completely respect that. In this case, no tracking script will be loaded. Only a single, technical cookie is set, which remembers that you have chosen "Accept necessary". This way, I don't have to ask you again on every page visit. Nothing else happens.
