Information

I released a Classic album at the end of 2020. This album contains 16 of my best tracks from Classic times. I can only suggest you to download this album. I put a lot of effort into the editing. I worked on it for days and nights. I edited every single track with the original Soundtracker from Classic times.

Why did I do that? I've always wanted a lot of my old tracks in a much better version. I didn't really own a good sound system and never had headphones at the time. And so it happened that I made many mistakes, which I have corrected today. More information can be found on the album page.

I don't want to offer any more old tracks here. There are just too many to list here. If you want more old tracks from me, just visit scene.org. Otherwise, get my Classic album, which I mentioned here.
