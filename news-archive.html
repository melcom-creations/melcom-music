‚ñ∫ melcom's chiptune archive now on scene.org

Almost 20 years ago, some demosceners and I got together with the idea to build a chiptune archive. We received incredible support from the community, especially from our IRC channel #coders.ger and the website back2roots.org. As more tracks were sent in, the archive grew, but it was discontinued shortly after I left the demoscene in 2004.

Only one snapshot of this archive from February 5, 2005, survived. It was backed up and eventually made its way to archive.org. Today, I'm thrilled to announce it has found a new, permanent home on scene.org!

melcom's ChipTune Archive (2005-02-05) contains:

  • Over 2,000 classic adlib modules
  • Over 4,200 classic chiptune modules
  • Over 1,700 classic ym modules

üíæ Download: melcom's_chiptune_archive_(2005-02-05).zip

I recommend XMPlay for listening, but you will need various plugins to play all the modules. If you have trouble finding them, just write to me, and I'll help you out.

A huge thank you to menace for making this possible by hosting the archive on Scene.org. Thanks, man!

Regards,
