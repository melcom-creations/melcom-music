Andreas Thomas Urban

.::: Short Version 😉
It all began in 1984 with a Commodore C-64 😋 For many years, I have been writing electronic music for the demo scene. After a break of more than 10 years, I started again. Over the course of my career, I have written a lot of music. Not everything has been released. If you want to know which software I use, follow this link. So, if you are interested in my work or have any questions, feel free to write me an email.
