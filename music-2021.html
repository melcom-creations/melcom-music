🔊 Track Info:

Headlock is the first track I composed in OpenMPT! It was a long journey. Schism Tracker has been with me for many years. And I love this music tracker!

Headlock is another example of what comes out when I suffer from my depression and my very strong self-doubt. The beginning of this track didn't quite work out for me, but I still decided to leave this part in the track. The beginning should simulate something weird, something broken. Like my brain, maybe... Approximately from 0:45min it gets quieter before the track really starts. I hope you like what comes next and how the whole track develops.
