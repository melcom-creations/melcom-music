🔊 Track Info:

"Crumble's Theme" is my first attempt at getting acquainted with FL Studio. This track is a unique blend of traditional tracking techniques from OpenMPT and an experimental evolution. The track was then further developed using FL Studio.

The background story of the track:
Crumble is a female character I create for my role-playing games like The Elder Scrolls Online, Baldur's Gate 3, Starfield, and many other RPGs. She tends to have a dark demeanor, but she's also humorous. Crumble has been accompanying me in my role-playing adventures for years. It's time she gets her own theme from me. Thanks, Crumble!
