🔊 Track Information

The work on "Desolate Runner 5: The Void Within" has been a very long journey. I spent nearly two years working on and refining this track, trying to keep the spirit and atmosphere of the previous entries alive while also pushing my sound further than ever before.

A large part of the track was created in OpenMPT, alongside Renoise and FL Studio, with many custom-made samples and instruments processed through various VST plugins.

Part 5 directly continues the events of "The Space We Are". And even though Part 4 is already six years old now (time really flies...), both tracks still share a lot of the same DNA.

Special thanks go to @Saga Musix for teaching me how to create my own custom samples in OpenMPT and push them even further with the help of VST plugins. 🙏

Desolate Runner is a sci-fi saga I began in 2017, told entirely through music. Over the years, multiple tracks have followed the dark and desperate journey of the "Runners."

This fifth and final chapter continues where "The Space We Are" ended - and finally brings the story to its conclusion.

The Desolate Runners - a small group of humanoids and aliens - struggle to survive in a distant future ruled by oppressive regimes.

Forced into hiding, constantly hunted, and hopelessly outnumbered, their existence has become a never-ending fight for survival.

Now, with their numbers fading and hope growing weaker, only a handful still believe in a better future - a place somewhere among the stars where life is no longer defined by fear, violence, and escape.

But the universe is cold and unforgiving.

Will they finally find freedom?
Or will the void consume what remains of them?

A happy ending feels far away.
But sometimes, the smallest spark can change everything.

Goodbye o/

🔊 Track Information

This track contains intentional audio glitches and sharp sound artifacts, so you may want to start with the volume slightly lower. The inspiration for these experimental elements came from Virgill and Console (Martin Gretschmann). Thank you both - I hope I managed to capture at least a little bit of your spirit and influence. 🙏

"Quantum Deception" has been an incredibly long and demanding journey. Every sample and instrument was rebuilt, reshaped, and remastered by me over and over again. Large parts of the track were originally created in OpenMPT and later exported as WAV stems - sometimes entire passages, sometimes just individual sounds or instruments. From there, the production evolved further in FL Studio and Renoise.

Countless VST plugins were used throughout the process, constantly transforming the sound into something darker, colder, and more atmospheric. By now, the project has moved far beyond traditional tracking in tools like OpenMPT or Schism Tracker. That's also the reason why no proper module file really exists anymore - and if one does, it's little more than an early sketch of what the track eventually became.

About the story behind the track:

The story takes place in a snowy Chicago far in the future. A private investigator - visually inspired by detectives from classic 1950s noir films - searches for his missing wife. It is his final case. Guided only by fragmented clues and fading hope, he moves through a city consumed by coldness, corruption, and shadows, desperately searching for answers while the world around him slowly falls apart.

A special thank-you goes to @Virgill for the feedback, the inspiration, and of course... for putting up with me 🙈
