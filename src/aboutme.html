🙂 About melcom

An insight into the creative mind behind the aliases. Discover my personal background, active pseudonyms, and a detailed breakdown of the digital workstations, tracker engines, hardware, and audio utilities I use to build my demoscene music and software projects.

Andreas Thomas Urban

It all began in 1984 with a Commodore C-64 😋 For many years, I have been writing electronic music for the demo scene. After a break of more than 10 years, I started again. Over the course of my career, I have written a lot of music. Not everything has been released.

If you want to know which software I use, follow this link. So, if you are interested in my work or have any questions, feel free to write me an email.

🔗 My Tools I Use

This is the primary set of tools and hardware I'm currently using. When I get more time (and don't forget), I plan to post a more comprehensive list covering everything I use for both music production and coding my tools. But for now, this will have to do.

Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) & Trackers

  • OpenMPT (Open-source audio module tracker)
  • FL Studio (Professional Digital Audio Workstation)
  • Schism Tracker (Open-source audio module tracker)
  • Impulse Tracker (Legacy module tracker)

Audio Editing

  • Ocenaudio (Audio editor with VST plugin support)

Audio Utilities & Encoders

  • melcom's FFmpeg Audio Normalizer (GUI Edition) (Audio volume normalizer tool)
  • Lame (Audio encoder)
  • Lame Front-End (GUI for LAME encoder)

Hardware & Playback

  • beyerdynamic DT 990 PRO (Headphones)
  • ESI - U22 XT (Professional 24-bit USB audio interface)
  • Dolby Access (Spatial sound for Windows 11)

🔗 My Favorite VST Plugins

I use the following VST plugins in conjunction with OpenMPT. I have and use many other plugins, but these are my absolute favorites!

Mastering & Dynamics

  • TDR Kotelnikov (Free mastering compressor)
  • TDR Nova (Free dynamic EQ)
  • TDR VOS SlickEQ (Free EQ)
  • Limiter №6 (Free mastering limiter)
  • PSP VintageWarmer2 (Paid multiband compressor)

Analysis Plugins

  • SPAN (Free spectrum analyzer)
  • Youlean Loudness Meter 2 ("Free" loudness meter)

Audio Effects

  • Electri-Q - or the posihfopit edition (Free EQ/Filter)
  • Ozone Imager V2 (Free stereo imager)
  • PaulXStretch (Free extreme time-stretch) - I use plugin version 1.2.4 with OpenMPT.
  • TP Basslane (Free bass management)
